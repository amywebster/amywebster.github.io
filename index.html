'Greed' Short Film

For this Gold Coast based film I worked as Production Designer. My role included breaking down the script, sourcing all props, sets and costumes needed - as well as setting each scene and coordinating props and costumes.

Interior re-design project

As part of my studies we were asked to re-design the entire Level 4 of QCAs Webb Centre. The large space had to hold multiple offices, create an open, shared environment & be environmentally forward.

HOP2HELP

I was apart of a group of students who created a guerilla marketing campaign to raise awareness for SPELD - learning difficulties. We designed a giant hopscotch and promoted our cause online through video.

B&W Film Photography

Earlier in the year I did a course in black and white, film photography. Using a Pentax K1000 we went through numerous rolls of films that we then developed (and printed) ourselves in the labs.

Latest Work

 

 

Hi! I am a Brisbane based, recent design graduate with education and experience in Interior Design, Production Design, Set Design and Animation. I have numerous films and theatre productions under my belt, as well as real world, interior design projects through the Queensland College of Art.

I mix my strong creativity with clever thinking to create smart and imaginative designs. I love exploring new areas, ideas and challenges. My other interests in Photography and Art influence my work and help make me a strong and skilled designer.

For more information please look at my resume.
